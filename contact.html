Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I register as a farmer with Improve Africa?

You can register as a farmer through our website, mobile app, or by visiting any of our regional offices. The registration process requires basic personal information, farm location, and details about your crops. Visit our Raw Materials page for more information.

What regions of Ghana does Improve Africa operate in?

Improve Africa currently operates in Greater Accra, Ashanti, Northern, Western, Eastern, and Volta regions. We are continuously expanding our operations to reach more farmers across Ghana. Visit our Regions page for detailed information about our operations in each region.

How does the payment process work for farmers?

Farmers receive payment directly to their mobile money accounts or bank accounts within 48 hours of collection. We offer multiple secure payment options including mobile money, bank transfers, and cash payments at our regional offices. Our pricing is transparent, and current prices for all commodities are published weekly.

How can businesses partner with Improve Africa?

Businesses can partner with us by contacting our Business Partnerships team at partners@improveafrica.org or by filling out the contact form on this page. We offer reliable supply of high-quality agricultural raw materials from across Ghana and can customize our services to meet your specific requirements.

What job opportunities are available at Improve Africa?

We offer various job opportunities across different departments including Field Operations, Administration, Training, Marketing, and more. Visit our Jobs page to see current openings and application instructions. You can also send your resume to careers@improveafrica.org for future opportunities.
