About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Improve Africa

Our Story

How Improve Africa began its journey

Improve Africa was founded in 2023 by a group of agricultural experts and social entrepreneurs who recognized the untapped potential of Ghana's agricultural sector. Having witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by farmers across Ghana, they were determined to create a platform that would empower farmers, particularly young people and women, with the resources, knowledge, and opportunities they needed to thrive.

What began as a small initiative in a single community is rapidly growing throughout Ghana, reaching farmers in various regions of the country. Our journey is guided by a simple yet powerful belief: that by investing in Ghana's farmers, we can create sustainable livelihoods, strengthen food security, and drive economic growth across the nation.

Today, Improve Africa continues to expand its reach and impact, working tirelessly to revolutionize agriculture in Ghana and improve the lives of farmers and their communities.

Our Mission

To reduce poverty and unemployment in Ghana through job creation in agriculture, supporting farmers with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities they need.

Improve Africa is committed to empowering farmers through education, mentorship, and access to markets for raw agricultural materials. We aim to reduce unemployment and increase economic opportunities through sustainable agriculture practices.

Our Vision

A prosperous Ghana where youth, especially women, thrive in agriculture, creating sustainable livelihoods and improving food security.

We envision a future where Ghana's agricultural sector is transformed through innovation, where farmers are respected entrepreneurs, and where agriculture is a driving force for economic growth and prosperity across the nation.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in enabling farmers to take control of their own futures.
  • Sustainability: We promote practices that protect the environment and ensure long-term viability.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies to solve agricultural challenges.
  • Inclusivity: We ensure that opportunities are accessible to all, regardless of gender, age, or background.

Our Milestones

Key achievements in our journey

December 2023

Founded Improve Africa with a pilot program in Accra, Ghana, supporting 50 local farmers.

February 2024

Established our first agricultural training center in Accra.

April 2024

Launched the Women in Agriculture initiative, providing specialized support to women farmers in Ghana.

June 2024

Expanded operations to the Northern Region of Ghana, bringing our resources to farmers in different ecological zones.

August 2024

Created our first international market connections, helping Ghanaian farmers access global markets for their produce.

October 2024

Launched digital farming platform, connecting Ghanaian farmers with resources, markets, and mentors through mobile technology.
