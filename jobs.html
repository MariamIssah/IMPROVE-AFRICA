Careers at Improve Africa

Join our mission to transform Ghana's agricultural sector and create sustainable livelihoods

At Improve Africa, we're committed to reducing unemployment by connecting young people with meaningful job opportunities in the agricultural sector. Our team consists of passionate individuals dedicated to empowering farmers and transforming agriculture across Ghana.

Whether you're looking to start your career, seeking a change, or wanting to make a positive impact, Improve Africa offers diverse roles that contribute to agricultural development and community empowerment. We value innovation, dedication, and a passion for sustainable development.

How to Apply

Simple steps to join our team

1

Find a Suitable Position

Browse our current openings and identify positions that match your skills, experience, and interests.

2

Prepare Your Application

Update your resume/CV and prepare a cover letter explaining why you're interested in the position and how your experience makes you a good fit.

3

Submit Your Application

Click the "Apply Now" button on the job listing and complete the online application form, attaching your resume/CV and cover letter.

4

Interview Process

If shortlisted, you'll be invited for interviews, which may include phone/video interviews and in-person meetings with the team.

5

Join Our Team

Successful candidates will receive an offer letter and join our team of passionate individuals working to transform agriculture in Ghana.

Important Notes:

  • All applications must be submitted through our online system.
  • Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
  • Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.
  • We encourage applications from women and individuals from diverse backgrounds.
  • For any questions about the application process, please contact careers@improveafrica.org.
