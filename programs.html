Our Programs

Empowering farmers and youth through mentorship, education, and job creation

Programs Overview

Transforming Ghana's agricultural landscape through targeted initiatives

At Improve Africa, we offer several comprehensive programs aimed at empowering farmers and youth across Ghana. Our initiatives focus on providing mentorship, education, and creating sustainable job opportunities within the agricultural sector.

Through our programs, we address the key challenges facing Ghana's agricultural sector: limited access to markets, knowledge gaps in modern farming techniques, and unemployment among rural youth. By combining practical training with real economic opportunities, we're building a stronger agricultural ecosystem that benefits farmers, communities, and the nation as a whole.

Each of our programs is designed with sustainability and scalability in mind, ensuring that the benefits continue to grow and spread throughout Ghana's agricultural communities.

Our Key Programs

Initiatives that drive agricultural transformation in Ghana

Farmer Mentorship Program

Our flagship mentorship program connects experienced agricultural experts with farmers across Ghana to improve farming techniques, expand businesses, and gain access to markets.

Key Components:

  • One-on-one mentorship with agricultural experts
  • Guidance on crop selection and rotation strategies
  • Business development and financial management training
  • Market access facilitation and networking opportunities
  • Sustainable farming practices and certification guidance

Program Duration:

6-month intensive mentorship with ongoing support

Eligibility:

Open to small and medium-scale farmers across Ghana

Youth Employment Program

Creating sustainable job opportunities for young Ghanaians in the agricultural value chain, with a special focus on empowering women in agriculture.

Key Components:

Stable income, skills development, career advancement opportunities

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our programs

How do I qualify for the Farmer Mentorship Program?

The Farmer Mentorship Program is open to all farmers in Ghana, regardless of experience level. To qualify, you need to be actively involved in farming, be willing to implement new techniques, and commit to the 6-month program duration. We particularly encourage applications from women and youth farmers.

Are there any costs associated with your programs?

Most of our programs are offered at no cost to participants, thanks to our partnerships with government agencies and international organizations. For some specialized training programs, there may be a nominal fee to cover materials and resources, but financial assistance is available for those who qualify.

How long do your programs typically last?

Program durations vary based on the type and intensity. The Farmer Mentorship Program runs for 6 months, while our Agricultural Education workshops typically last 1-2 weeks. The Youth Employment Program offers ongoing job placements with regular check-ins and support. All programs include follow-up support after completion.

Can I participate in multiple programs simultaneously?

Yes, many of our participants engage in multiple programs simultaneously. For example, you might participate in the Farmer Mentorship Program while also attending Agricultural Education workshops. We encourage this integrated approach as it provides a more comprehensive support system for your agricultural journey.

How can I apply for your programs?

You can apply for our programs through our website by clicking the "Register Now" button, visiting any of our regional offices across Ghana, or calling our program hotline at +233 30 273 9900. Our team is available to assist with the application process and answer any questions you might have.

Do you offer programs in all regions of Ghana?

We currently operate in several regions of Ghana, including Accra, Northern Region, and Volta Region, with plans to expand to additional regions. Even if we don't have a physical presence in your region yet, many of our educational resources are available online, and we can connect you with mentors remotely.
